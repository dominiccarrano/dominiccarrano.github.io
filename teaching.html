Teaching

One of the best things about UC Berkeley especially here in my home department of EECS, is its vibrant teaching community which I'm grateful to be a part of. I'm currently serving as the Head TA for EE 120, Signals and Systems, and have been on the course staff for four semesters now.

As a TA, I've focused on example-driven learning by showing modern applications to students, especially through the use of interactive tools such as the Jupyter Notebook platform. As head TA, I've spearheaded, over the course of three semesters, a project to bring a full set of 10 Jupyter Notebook virtual labs to the class. I'm grateful to have worked with a fantastic staff toward this goal, and we published the work in the 2020 International Conference on Higher Education Advances (pdf here).
