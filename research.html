Research

I'm broadly interested in distributed computing, statistics, optimization, signal processing, and electrical engineering and computer science education.

As an undergraduate researcher, I've worked at the intersection of many of these areas, investigating how to accelerate machine learning tasks through the use of distributed computing and error-correcting codes.
